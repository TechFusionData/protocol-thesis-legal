Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Protocol Thesis ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address, if you subscribe to our newsletter via our website (protocol-thesis.beehiiv.com), through our newsletter platform Beehiiv.
  • Anonymous usage data when you visit our website or watch our videos, including page views, referral source, country/region, device type, and approximate session duration. This is collected by Beehiiv (for our website) and by YouTube, TikTok, and X (for our channels on those platforms) through their own analytics.
  • Comments and engagement signals you voluntarily post on our YouTube, TikTok, or X content. Those are subject to the platform's own terms.

We do not collect or process payment information; we do not currently sell paid subscriptions.

2. How We Use Information

  • Send you newsletter issues you have subscribed to.
  • Measure aggregate audience engagement and improve our content.
  • Respond to inquiries you send us.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to deliver the Services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Beehiiv (newsletter, website hosting, email delivery): beehiiv.com/privacy
  • YouTube (video hosting): policies.google.com/privacy
  • TikTok (short-form video distribution): tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • X / Twitter (social posting): x.com/en/privacy

When you interact with our content on third-party platforms, those platforms collect data subject to their own policies.

3a. PT Publisher (Our Publishing Tool)

PT Publisher is our internal tool that posts our own videos to our own social media accounts (including TikTok) through each platform's official API, after we explicitly authorize it. It accesses only our own account's basic profile (username, avatar, account ID) and the ability to upload videos to our own account. It does not access, collect, or store any data about other users of those platforms. It does not read comments, messages, follower lists, or any third-party information. API access tokens are stored encrypted on our own equipment, are never shared, and can be revoked by us at any time in the platform's settings. Posts made by PT Publisher carry the platform's AI-generated-content disclosure where applicable, consistent with our public AI-narration disclosure (narration is AI-generated; scripts, research, and editorial are human).

4. Cookies

Our Beehiiv-hosted website uses cookies and similar technologies to provide functionality (e.g., remembering subscription state) and to measure aggregate traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time (every email contains an unsubscribe link)
  • Object to processing or request data portability

To exercise these rights, contact techfusioncontent@gmail.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address only while you remain subscribed. Unsubscribing removes you from active mailing; we may retain anonymized log records for a limited period for fraud and abuse prevention.

7. International Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than your own. Where applicable, transfers are governed by standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

8. Children

Our Services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: techfusioncontent@gmail.com.
